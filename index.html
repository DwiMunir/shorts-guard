Privacy Policy for Shorts Guard

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data our Chrome extension, Shorts Guard, collects and how we use and protect that data.

Information We Collect

Shorts Guard does not collect any personal data from users. We do not track, store, or share any personal information.

Permissions Explanation

To function correctly, Shorts Guard requires the following permissions:

  • Scripting: Allows the extension to inject JavaScript into YouTube pages to hide Shorts content.
  • ActiveTab: Provides temporary access to the currently active tab to modify YouTube content when the user interacts with the extension.
  • Tabs: Accesses information about browser tabs to determine if the active tab is a YouTube page.
  • <all_urls>: Ensures flexibility for future updates and functionality across various YouTube page instances.
  • Host Permissions: Allows interaction with all HTTP and HTTPS URLs to ensure the extension functions across all YouTube pages.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Shorts Guard does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, ensuring complete privacy and security for users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
